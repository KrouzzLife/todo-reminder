Enable Reminders (iPhone/Mac/Android)

Internet connection required for notifications to queue and deliver.

  • iPhone (Safari): Share > Add to Home Screen (PWA) > Settings > Notifications > Allow for this app (banners with sound).
  • Mac (Chrome): System Settings > Notifications > Chrome > Allow banners with sound/vibration.
  • Android (Chrome): Chrome Settings > Site settings > Notifications > Allow for this site (high priority).
